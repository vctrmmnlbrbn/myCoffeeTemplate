Our Philosophy

Coffee is a ritual,
not a routine.

We source our beans directly from farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships that go beyond trade. Every roast is calibrated to unlock the unique character of each origin.

From the first grind to the final pour, our baristas treat every cup as a small act of craftsmanship.
